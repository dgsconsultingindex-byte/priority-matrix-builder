Complete Slack Integration Guide

Step-by-step instructions to connect PrioBox with your Slack workspace

‚è±Ô∏è Estimated Time: 15-20 minutes

What You'll Achieve

By the end of this guide, you'll have:

  • Automatic Slack notifications when tasks are added to PrioBox
  • Real-time updates for your entire team
  • Customizable notification formats
  • Optional filters for high-priority items only

Prerequisites

  • Access to a Slack workspace (free or paid)
  • Permission to add apps to your Slack workspace
  • PrioBox data source (Google Sheets, Airtable, or similar)
  • 10-20 minutes of uninterrupted time

Part 1: Create Your Zapier Account

Step 1.1: Sign Up for Zapier

  1. Go to https://zapier.com
  2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Verify your email (check your inbox)
  5. Sign in to your new Zapier account

üí° Tip: Use Your Work Email

Sign up with the same email you use for Slack and Google Workspace. This makes connecting accounts easier later.

Part 2: Set Up Your First Zap

Step 2.1: Create a New Zap

  1. Click the orange "+ Create" button (left sidebar or top right)
  2. You'll see the Zap editor with two main sections: Trigger and Action
  3. At the top, rename your Zap to "PrioBox ‚Üí Slack Notifications"
üì∏ Screenshot: Zapier dashboard with "+ Create" button highlighted

Part 3: Configure the Trigger (When This Happens...)

Step 3.1: Choose Trigger App

  1. Click on the "Trigger" section
  2. Search for "Google Sheets" (or your data source)
  3. Click on "Google Sheets"

Step 3.2: Choose Trigger Event

  1. Select "New Spreadsheet Row" from the dropdown
  2. Click "Continue"

Step 3.3: Connect Google Account

  1. Click "Sign in to Google Sheets"
  2. A popup window will appear
  3. Choose your Google account
  4. Click "Allow" to give Zapier permission to access your sheets
  5. The popup closes and you return to Zapier

Step 3.4: Configure Trigger Settings

  1. Drive: Select "My Google Drive"
  2. Spreadsheet: Find and select your PrioBox spreadsheet
  3. Worksheet: Select the sheet tab (usually "Sheet1")
  4. Trigger Column: Leave blank (optional field)
  5. Click "Continue"

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Make Sure You Have Data

Your Google Sheet should have at least one row of sample data before testing. Zapier needs to see your column structure.

Step 3.5: Test the Trigger

  1. Click "Test trigger"
  2. Zapier will pull the most recent row from your sheet
  3. You should see all your columns displayed (Task Name, Priority, etc.)
  4. If successful, click "Continue"
üì∏ Screenshot: Successful trigger test showing sample data

Part 4: Configure the Action (Do This...)

Step 4.1: Choose Action App

  1. Click the "+" button or "Action" section
  2. Search for "Slack"
  3. Click on "Slack"

Step 4.2: Choose Action Event

  1. Select "Send Channel Message"
  2. Click "Continue"

Step 4.3: Connect Slack Account

  1. Click "Sign in to Slack"
  2. A popup appears showing your Slack workspaces
  3. Choose the workspace where you want notifications
  4. Click "Allow" to give Zapier permission
  5. Return to Zapier

Step 4.4: Design Your Notification Message

This is where you customize what appears in Slack:

  1. Channel: Select the channel (e.g., #priorities or #team-updates)
  2. Message Text: Use the template below

How to insert data from your sheet:

  1. Click in the "Message Text" field
  2. You'll see a "+ Insert Data" button appear
  3. Click it to see all your Google Sheets columns
  4. Click on a column name to insert it
  5. It appears as a bubble/pill (e.g., "1. Task Name")
  6. Repeat for each piece of data you want to include

Step 4.5: Optional Settings

  1. Bot Name: Enter "PrioBox" (this appears as the sender)
  2. Bot Icon: Enter an emoji like :dart: or :chart_with_upwards_trend:
  3. Leave other settings as default
  4. Click "Continue"

Part 5: Test and Activate

Step 5.1: Send Test Notification

  1. Click "Test action" button
  2. Zapier sends a test message to your Slack channel
  3. Go to Slack and check the channel - you should see it!
  4. If you see the message, click "Continue"

‚ö†Ô∏è Troubleshooting: No Message in Slack?

  • Check you selected the correct channel
  • Verify you're looking at the right Slack workspace
  • Refresh Slack or check desktop app (not just browser)
  • Make sure the channel is public or you've invited the bot

Step 5.2: Activate Your Zap

  1. Look for the toggle switch in the top right corner
  2. Click it to turn your Zap "ON"
  3. You'll see a confirmation message
  4. Your Zap is now live! üéâ

Step 5.3: Test with Real Data

  1. Go to your Google Sheet
  2. Add a new row with test task data
  3. Wait 1-5 minutes (Zapier checks every 15 minutes on free tier)
  4. Check Slack - you should receive a notification!

Part 6: Advanced Customization (Optional)

Option 1: Filter for High-Priority Only

Add a Filter Step

  2. Choose "Filter"
  3. Set condition: "Priority Level" ‚Üí "Equals" ‚Üí "High"
  4. Save and test
  5. Now only high-priority tasks trigger notifications!

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Zap isn't triggering

  • Verify your Zap is turned ON (toggle in top right)
  • Check that new rows are actually being added to your sheet
  • Remember: Free tier checks every 15 minutes, not instantly
  • Look at "Zap History" in Zapier dashboard for error logs

Issue: Messages not formatting correctly

  • Make sure you're inserting data fields, not typing column names
  • Test the trigger again to refresh available fields
  • Check for typos in your message template
  • Use Slack markdown syntax (*bold*, _italic_)

Issue: Too many notifications

  • Add a Filter step to limit when notifications are sent
  • Consider batching: send daily digest instead of each task
  • Use different channels for different priority levels

Issue: Running out of Zapier tasks

  • Free tier: 100 tasks/month
  • Each notification = 1 task
  • If you exceed: Upgrade to Starter plan ($19.99/month)
  • Or: Be more selective with filters
